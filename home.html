About

Welcome! My name is Magda but you can call me Adot. I'm 22 years old and I've got bachelor's degree in graphic design. I've been drawing and painting not only digitally but on paper as well since I was 10 years old. I like drawing my original characters the most - I have a head full of ideas when it comes to storylines. I'm interested in many things: games (especially Red Dead Redemption 2, Genshin Impact or Assassin's Creed series), anime, true crime stories, fantasy books etc. If you are interested, some of my works are available to the public view on my social media where you can follow me.
